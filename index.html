My Story

Producer Kagoh is the founder of Kanairo Classic Records, a Nairobi-based music production company dedicated to creating authentic African sounds with global appeal. With over a decade of experience in the music industry, Kagoh has worked with some of the biggest names in East African music.

Kanairo Classic Records

Founded in 2015, Kanairo Classic Records has become a hub for talented artists and producers. Our mission is to preserve and modernize traditional African sounds while creating music that resonates with audiences worldwide.

About Producer Kagoh

Producer Kagoh is a visionary music producer and the founder of Kanairo Classic Records, based in Nairobi, Kenya. With over a decade of experience in the music industry, Kagoh has established himself as one of East Africa's most innovative producers, blending traditional African sounds with contemporary global music trends.

His work has been featured on major platforms and he has collaborated with some of the biggest names in African music. Kagoh's mission is to preserve and modernize authentic African sounds while creating music that resonates with audiences worldwide.

Key Achievements

  • Founded Kanairo Classic Records in 2015
  • Produced over 100 tracks for various artists
  • Featured on major streaming platforms
  • Collaborated with top East African artists
  • Received multiple industry awards and nominations
